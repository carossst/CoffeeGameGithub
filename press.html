Brew or False is a privacy-first coffee quiz for curious players, built for mobile and desktop with no account required.

Overview

Brew or False is a fast true-or-false game about coffee. It covers brewing, drinks, beans, culture, pricing, and common misconceptions.

The goal is simple: respond quickly, learn something concrete, and keep moving.

Key facts

Launch February 2026
Product Brew or False, a coffee-themed true or false quiz
Model Free to play, with optional one-time premium access
Content 201 handcrafted coffee questions with short explanations
Platform Browser, mobile and desktop, installable as a Progressive Web App
Privacy No account required, no ads, no third-party analytics

How it works

Players see one statement at a time and decide whether it is true or false.

Each run allows three mistakes. Feedback is immediate and includes a short explanation designed for quick comprehension.

A premium mode unlocks full access and replay value without subscriptions.
